Importing sheet music

Aria reads MusicXML files: .musicxml, .xml, or compressed .mxl.

  • musescore.com — open a score, tap Download → MusicXML, save it to the Files app, then tap Import here. Free for public-domain and original scores.
  • MuseScore app (free, Mac/PC) — File → Export → MusicXML.
  • Printed music or PDFs — convert with the free Audiveris scanner first, correct mistakes in MuseScore, then export MusicXML.

Imported songs are stored on this device and stay in your song list.
