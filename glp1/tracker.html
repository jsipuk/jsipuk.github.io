Progress tracker

Log your dose, weight, measurements and notes week by week. Everything you type is saved only in this browser on this device — nothing is uploaded anywhere. Use Data → Save a backup now and then so you don't lose it, and Print to keep a paper copy (or print it blank to fill in by hand).

Tip: keep progress photos in a private album on your phone — not here.
