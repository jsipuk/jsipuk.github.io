About

I'm a Consulting Sales Engineer at Netskope with 5+ years helping organisations translate complex security and networking requirements into practical, outcome-driven solutions. I specialise in SASE, SSE, and Zero Trust — working across technical and non-technical audiences in enterprises of all sizes, across most industries and global regions.

My work spans presales, deep-dive architecture sessions, proof of concepts, partner training, and customer demos. I'm passionate about making modern security approachable and helping people understand how converged platforms can solve real business problems.
