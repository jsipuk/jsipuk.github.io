How to Play

The Ruler of Three Faces is a text-driven role-playing story. You read, you decide, and the kingdom remembers.

  • Suggested choices appear as buttons beneath the story. They are useful — never exhaustive.
  • Free actions: type anything into “What do you do?” — question people, search things, try the unreasonable. The world answers in kind, and it will not simply agree with the impossible.
  • People remember. Kindness, threats and cleverness all leave marks on how characters treat you.
  • The panels hold your inventory, the people you have met, your quests, a chronicle-style journal, a codex of discovered lore, and your achievements — some hidden.
  • Saving: the game autosaves every turn. Manual slots, JSON export/import and a full story transcript live behind the 💾 button.

One more thing: the gatekeeper will never reveal the password. That has never once stopped anyone worth admitting.
